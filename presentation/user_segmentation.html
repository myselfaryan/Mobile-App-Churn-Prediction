User Segmentation Insights

Segment Overview

K-means clustering identified 5 distinct user segments with varying churn rates ranging from 27.8% to 29.4%.

Segment Characteristics

Segment 3 shows the lowest churn rate (27.8%) and has higher engagement metrics, including more sessions per week and longer session durations.

Subscription Distribution

Premium and VIP subscribers are more prevalent in Segments 1 and 3, correlating with lower churn rates in these segments.

Targeting Strategy

Segmentation enables personalized retention strategies based on user behavior patterns and preferences specific to each group.
