Problem Statement & Business Context

The Mobile App Churn Challenge

Mobile apps typically lose 70-80% of their users within the first 90 days after installation.

Business Impact

High churn rates directly impact revenue, user acquisition costs, and overall app sustainability.

Prediction & Prevention

Identifying at-risk users before they churn allows for targeted retention strategies and improved ROI.

Data-Driven Approach

Leveraging user behavior data, demographics, and engagement metrics to predict and reduce churn.
