Data Analysis & Key Findings

Overall Churn Rate

Analysis revealed an overall churn rate of 28.8%, indicating significant user retention challenges.

Subscription Impact

Free users have a higher churn rate compared to Premium and VIP users, suggesting subscription tier influences retention.

Engagement Correlation

Users with lower session counts and shorter session durations are significantly more likely to churn.

Key Retention Factors

Push notification settings, feature usage count, and recency of activity are strong predictors of user retention.
