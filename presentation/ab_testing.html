A/B Testing Results

Tested Interventions

Three retention strategies were tested: Personalized Notifications, Improved Onboarding, and Feature Recommendations.

Most Effective Strategy

Improved Onboarding showed the highest churn reduction at 3.73%, representing a significant improvement over the control group.

Engagement Improvements

The improved onboarding intervention also increased average sessions per week by 0.35 sessions, the highest engagement boost among all strategies.

Statistical Significance

All interventions showed statistically significant results p < 0.001 with improved onboarding having the strongest effect size.
